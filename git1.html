GitHub Basic: Building Your Coding Portfolio

September 24, 2023
North South University, Lib 607

Organized by:

IEEE North South University Student Branch
IEEE NSU Industry Application Society
IEEE NSU Robotics & Automation Society


September 24, 2023, I served as an Instructor for a workshop titled "GitHub Basics: Building Your Coding Portfolio" at North South University. This event, organized by the IEEE North South University Student Branch and the IEEE NSU Industry Application Society, focused on fundamental GitHub skills. I provided hands-on training to participants, aiming to enhance their ability to manage and showcase their coding projects effectively. The workshop emphasized practical applications and best practices, empowering students to create and maintain impressive coding portfolios.
